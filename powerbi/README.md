## Power BI Dashboard

This folder contains the Power BI dashboard used to visualize key insights from the Brazilian Olist e-commerce dataset.

### Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to identify the primary drivers of customer satisfaction by combining insights from delivery performance, pricing, and product categories.

### Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** $17.13M
* **Total Orders:** 98K
* **Average Customer Rating:** 4.03
* **Late Delivery Rate:** 6.3%

---

### Key Analyses

#### 1. Delivery Performance vs Customer Ratings

* On-time deliveries receive significantly higher review scores than late deliveries.
* Delivery performance is a major factor in customer satisfaction.

#### 2. Order Value vs Customer Ratings

* Higher-value orders tend to receive slightly lower review scores.
* Customer expectations may increase with order value.

#### 3. Combined Impact: Delivery + Order Value

* Late deliveries have a stronger negative impact on high-value orders.
* High-paying customers are more sensitive to delays.

#### 4. Product Category Performance

* High-revenue categories do not always correspond to high customer satisfaction.
* Some categories may require operational or quality improvements.

---

### Filters

* **Delivery Status:** Filter between on-time and late orders
* **Product Category:** Filter by product category

---

### Key Insight

> Delivery delays are the primary driver of customer dissatisfaction, especially for high-value orders.
